Where I've lived

I grew up in Jamaica, Queens until the age of 12. I lived with both my parents until I was 5 then I just lived with my dad. When I was 12 my sister and I moved in with my mom and her boyfriend in a basement in Ozone Park. Then, I moved to my aunt's house in the Bronx with my sister and my mom. Then, I moved to East New York with my mom, sister, and my mom's boyfriend. Then I moved into my uncle's house near Prospect Park with my sister and my mom. Then, I moved to Midwood, Brooklyn with my sister, my mom, and her boyfriend, which is where I live now.


Favorite Foods!

I love food from all types of countries, but I especially love Ecuadorean food and East Asian food.

Both my parents are Ecuadorean, so I grew up with Ecuadorean food, but my favorite dish is arroz con frijoles con platano con carne asada!

One of my all time favorite foods is platano con queso. I've been eating this since I was a baby and I always ask my mom to make it because no one can make it like her.


Another one of my favorite Ecuadrean foods is seco de pollo.

When I'm not eating Latin food, I usually eat East Asian food, especially Japanese and Chinese food.

I especially love sushi. I used to hate sushi, but now I always order sushi when I can. My two favorite rolls are california rolls and spicy tuna rolls.
